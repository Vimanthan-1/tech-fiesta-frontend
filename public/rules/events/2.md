# Tech Survivor – Elimination Arena Rule Book

## Event Overview
Tech Survivor is an individual technical competition designed to test participants' technical knowledge, logical thinking, programming concepts, and problem-solving skills through multiple competitive rounds.

---

## Eligibility
- Individual participation only
- Admin-approved registration
- One account per participant

---

## Requirements
- Laptop / Desktop System
- Stable Internet Connection
- Updated Web Browser
- Valid College ID
- Admin Approval

---

## Event Stages

### Stage 1: Technical Quiz
- Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs)

### Stage 2: Problem Solving
- Algorithmic & Coding Challenge

### Stage 3: Rapid Fire
- Tie-breaker round (if required)

---

## Rules & Regulations
- Report before the event starts.
- One attempt per question.
- Submitted answers cannot be changed.
- No tab switching or browser minimization.
- No multiple accounts or devices.
- Maintain fair play throughout the event.
- Follow all instructions from the organizers.
- Admin's decision is final.

---

## Scoring
- Automatic Evaluation
- Live Leaderboard
- Winners are decided based on score and completion time.

---

## Disqualification
- Cheating or unfair practices.
- Multiple rule violations.
- Sharing login credentials.
- Misconduct during the event.

---

## Declaration
"By participating in Tech Survivor, every participant agrees to follow all event rules and maintain fairness throughout the competition."
