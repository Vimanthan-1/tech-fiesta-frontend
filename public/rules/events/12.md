# Tech Fiesta – ADZAP Rule Book

## Event Overview
The **ADZAP** event is a creative advertising contest in which teams are tested on their convincing capabilities, marketing pitch, and performance while trying to sell a wacky, unusual, or nonexistent product.

---

## Eligibility
- Open to undergraduate and postgraduate students.
- This is a group event with exactly 4 members per team.
- Every team must register before the event begins.

---

## Rules & Guidelines
1. The product name or concept will be given to the team 10 minutes before their performance slot.
2. The total time allotted for each performance is 3 to 5 minutes. Teams must stay within this time limit.
3. Vulgarity, double entendres, or offensive terms/actions are strictly prohibited and will lead to immediate disqualification.
4. Use of props and background music is allowed and encouraged to enhance the performance.
5. Teams must report to the venue on time.

---

## Judging Criteria
- **Humor Factor:** How entertaining and engaging the performance is.
- **Team Dynamics:** Coordination, active participation, and chemistry among team members.
- **Pertinence to Topic:** How well the team sticks to the assigned product/concept.
- **Creativity:** Originality of the advertisement script, pitch, and execution.
- **Impact:** Overall effectiveness and convincing power of the product pitch.

---

## General Rules
- The decision of the judges and organizing committee will be final and binding.
- Any misconduct or violation of campus discipline will result in disqualification.
