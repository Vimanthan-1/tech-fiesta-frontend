# Tech Fiesta – Missing Lyrics Rule Book

## Event Overview
**Missing Lyrics – Musical Fun Challenge** is a fun-filled team event where participants must identify and complete missing song lyrics from popular tracks or recognize songs from background music tracks.

---

## Eligibility
- The event is open to undergraduate and postgraduate students.
- This is a team event with a maximum of 4 members per team.
- Every team must register before the event begins.

---

## Event Format & Rounds
The competition is conducted in two main rounds:
- **Round 1 – Basic Lyrics Round:** Teams answer simple lyric-based questions or fill in missing portions of played songs.
- **Round 2 – BGM Challenge:** Teams are played a background music (BGM) track without lyrics and must identify the correct song, movie, or music track.

---

## Rules & Guidelines
1. Teams must answer within the given time limit for each question.
2. The use of mobile phones, smartwatches, or any external help is strictly prohibited.
3. A buzzer system will be used to determine which team gets to respond first.
4. Teams must maintain discipline and avoid shouting out answers out of turn.
5. In case of a tie, a tie-breaker round will be conducted.

---

## Judging Criteria
- **Correctness:** Accuracy of the identified lyrics/song/movie.
- **Speed:** Quickness of response (via buzzer).
- **Team Coordination:** Effective teamwork and consensus.

---

## General Rules
- Any form of cheating or using unauthorized devices will result in immediate disqualification.
- The event coordinator's decision will be final and binding on all participants.
