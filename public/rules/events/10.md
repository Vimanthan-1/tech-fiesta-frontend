# Tech Fiesta – Murder Mystery Rule Book

## Event Overview
**Murder Mystery – Investigation Challenge** is an interactive team-based event where participants solve clues, investigate suspects, and uncover the mystery behind a fictional crime scene.

---

## Eligibility
- The event is open to undergraduate and postgraduate students.
- This is a team event with a maximum of 4 members per team.
- Every team must register before the event begins.

---

## Event Format
- **Round 1 – Clue Hunt:** Teams collect and solve hidden clues scattered within the designated areas.
- **Final Presentation:** Teams present their final investigation report, explain the motive, and identify the culprit.

---

## Rules & Guidelines
1. Teams must stay within the assigned event boundaries and designated investigation areas.
2. Clues should not be damaged, hidden from other teams, or relocated.
3. Participants must maintain fair play. Cooperation between different teams is strictly prohibited.
4. Time limits for both clue collection and final presentation must be followed strictly.

---

## Judging Criteria
- **Accuracy of Investigation:** Correctly identifying the culprit, motive, and sequence of events.
- **Teamwork & Coordination:** Collaboration and utilization of team members' strengths.
- **Problem-Solving Ability:** Logic and deduction used to decipher complex clues.
- **Completion Time:** Speed of completing the investigation.

---

## General Rules
- Any form of misconduct, sabotage of clues, or cheating will lead to immediate disqualification.
- The organizers hold the final authority in all decisions and disputes.
