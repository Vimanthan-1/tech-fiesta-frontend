# Tech Fiesta – Hack The Campus Rule Book

## Event Overview
**HACK THE CAMPUS** is an interactive cyber-themed treasure hunt that combines technology, logical thinking, cybersecurity, and problem-solving into an exciting campus adventure.

Participants will navigate through multiple challenge levels by scanning QR codes placed across the campus. Each QR code unlocks a unique task involving coding, cryptography, logical puzzles, cybersecurity concepts, or AI-based challenges. Teams must solve each challenge within the given time limit to progress to the next level.

The event is designed to test analytical thinking, teamwork, technical knowledge, and time management while creating a fun and competitive learning experience.

---

## Eligibility
- Open to undergraduate and postgraduate students.
- Team Event (2–4 members per team).
- Every team must register before the event begins.

---

## Event Structure
The event consists of multiple levels. Each level contains:
- QR Code Challenge
- Technical Puzzle
- Logical Reasoning
- Cybersecurity Task
- Coding Challenge
- Hidden Clues

Teams must successfully complete the current level to unlock the next one.

---

## General Rules
1. Every team must register before the event.
2. Each team will receive a unique Team ID.
3. Teams are allowed a maximum of 25 minutes to complete the event.
4. The countdown timer starts immediately after Level 1 begins.
5. Teams must scan the QR code provided at each checkpoint to access the next challenge.
6. Skipping levels is strictly prohibited.
7. Teams must solve the current challenge before moving to the next level.
8. Internet access is permitted only if explicitly mentioned for a challenge.
9. Any attempt to manipulate the website, database, timer, or leaderboard will result in immediate disqualification.
10. Participants must not damage, remove, or relocate any QR code placed on campus.
11. Teams must follow campus rules and avoid disturbing other participants.
12. The decision of the organizers and judges will be final and binding.

## Time Rules
- **Total Event Duration:** 25 Minutes
- The timer starts once Level 1 is accessed.
- The timer continues across every level.
- No extra time will be provided.
- In case of a tie, the team with the faster completion time will be ranked higher.

---

## Disqualification
A team may be disqualified for:
- Attempting to skip levels.
- Sharing challenge answers with other teams.
- Tampering with QR codes.
- Using unfair means or unauthorized software.
- Creating fake registrations.
- Misconduct or violation of campus discipline.

---

## Code of Conduct
- Respect fellow participants, volunteers, and organizers.
- Maintain fair play throughout the competition.
- Follow all safety instructions while moving across campus.
- Report any technical issue immediately to the organizing team.
- Any abusive or inappropriate behavior will lead to immediate disqualification.
