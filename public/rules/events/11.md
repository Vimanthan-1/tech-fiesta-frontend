# Tech Fiesta – Wiki Surfers Rule Book

## Event Overview
**Wiki Surfers – Wikipedia Navigation Challenge** is a fast-paced internet navigation challenge where participants race from a starting Wikipedia article to a target Wikipedia article using only internal Wikipedia hyperlinks. Teams must think strategically, connect topics quickly, and reach the target page in the least number of clicks and shortest time possible.

---

## Eligibility
- Open to undergraduate and postgraduate students.
- This is a team event with a maximum of 2 members per team.
- Every team must register before the event begins.

---

## Event Format
- **Round 1 – Speed Navigation:** Teams navigate between randomly assigned Wikipedia pages using only internal links.
- **Round 2 – Final Sprint:** Top qualified teams compete with more difficult target pages under stricter time limits.

---

## Rules & Guidelines
1. Only internal Wikipedia hyperlinks within the articles may be used to navigate.
2. The use of external search engines (like Google), browser page search (Ctrl+F), or opening links in new tabs is strictly prohibited.
3. Participants must maintain fair play. Sharing routes or receiving external assistance is not allowed.
4. Time limits for each round must be followed strictly.

---

## Judging Criteria
- **Least Number of Clicks:** Fewer clicks to reach the target page is prioritized.
- **Fastest Completion Time:** In case of a tie in clicks, the faster team wins.
- **Navigation Strategy:** Efficiency of the path chosen.
- **Team Coordination:** Communication and decision-making within the team.

---

## General Rules
- Any use of unfair means, external tools, search functionality, or browser extensions will lead to immediate disqualification.
- The organizing committee holds the final authority in all decisions.
