# Tech Fiesta – Best Meme Creation Rule Book

## Event Overview
Best Meme Creation is a solo competition where participants are required to create the funniest, most creative meme based on the day's events at Tech Fiesta. It tests your humor, speed, and design skills under time pressure.

---

## Eligibility
- The event is open to undergraduate and postgraduate students.
- This is an individual (solo) event.
- Each participant can register only once for the event.

---

## Rules & Guidelines
1. The meme must be original and created entirely by the participant.
2. The meme content must be directly related to the Tech Fiesta events happening on the same day.
3. Participants can use any tools, apps, or websites for meme creation.
4. Content must be appropriate, clean, and respectful. Offensive, abusive, or inappropriate material will lead to immediate disqualification.
5. Each participant can submit only one meme.
6. The meme must be submitted within the given time limit.

---

## Judging Criteria
- **Creativity & Originality:** Unique concept and clever humor.
- **Relevance:** How well the meme reflects the events of the day at Tech Fiesta.
- **Humor & Impact:** Funniness and general impact of the meme.
- **Presentation & Clarity:** Readability, editing quality, and visual clarity.

---

## Code of Conduct
- Maintain professional behavior and sportsmanship.
- Respect all fellow participants, organizers, and judges.
- Plagiarism or submitting pre-made memes will result in disqualification.

---

## Additional Notes
- The specific submission method (e.g., Google Form, WhatsApp, etc.) will be shared by the coordinators during the event.
- Late submissions will not be considered under any circumstances.
- The decision of the judges will be final and binding.
